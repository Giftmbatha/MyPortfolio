About

What I Care About

I believe good software starts with clear thinking. I'm drawn to backend development because it's where logic meets practicality—transforming requirements into reliable systems that serve users without drawing attention to themselves.

How I Approach Learning

I learn by building, then reflecting. Each project teaches me something about trade-offs: when to optimize, when to keep things simple, and how to make systems understandable for the next person who works on them.

What I Value in Systems
